Food Safety Standards

The market today is flooded with a variety of food products and as consumers we have a wide range of products and brands to choose from. Are we aware of where and how the products we buy are being grown or manufactured? We hear enough about unethical practices being followed by growers and suppliers of food products resulting in sub-standard quality of foods. We hear about severe nutritional and health issues arising from the consumption of unsafe and poor quality foods. But we have no way to determine what is right for us.

Safety and Quality Practices

At Sahyadri Farms, we respect you as a conscious and discerning customer. We know that you care about what you eat, how it is grown, and whether or not it is healthy and safe for you. We want you to be aware and informed. And so, we are determined to keep food quality and safety as our core value and priority by being committed to delivering quality, safe & traceable products, as per the national and international standards requirements.

Quality and Food Safety Policy

Certifications for food quality and safety

The proof of our food quality is demonstrated through various certifications we have received from authorities in India and all over the world. These certifications are awarded based on the results of rigorous tests, inspections and audits. Our products are subject to stringent tests of quality and safety and are recognized as safe foods that come straight from the farm to your plate.

Certifications and Licenses we have received till date

FSSAI

Food Safety and Standards Authority of India

Sahyadri Farms adheres to the regulations and standards set by FSSAI by ensuring food safety at our farms. Food Safety Standard Authority of India is responsible for protecting and promoting public health through these regulation and supervision of food safety.

APEDA

Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority

We adhere to standards set for all pack houses handling and processing horticulture produce viz fresh fruits, vegetables etc., for export. We as exporters meet the international standards in-terms of quality of produce with quarantine safety.

FSSC

Food Safety System Certification

The FSSC 22000 v5 certificate proves that the organizations food safety management system is in compliance with the Scheme requirements.

BRCGS

Brand Reputation through Compliance Global Standard

Recognised by the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI), BRC Global Standards specify requirements for organizations. This standard specifies that Sahyadri Farms adhere to food safety, quality and operational criteria required within a food manufacturing organization. Our products are safe, legal and of high quality.

Global G.A.P.

Global Good Agricultural Practices

Through this certification, we demonstrate our adherence to GLOBAL G.A.P. standards that are guidelines developed by the food industry, producers' organizations, governments and NGOs, aiming to codify agricultural practices at farm level for a range of commodities.

Fair Trade

Fair trade is all about better prices, good working conditions, and sustainability for farmers and workers at Sahyadri Farms. We have established a fair trade platform where Agro Input companies can directly meet farmers for trade - a revolution in the interest of farmers. With no middlemen involved, farmers get the products of the right quality at a fair price.

Tesco

Sahyadri Farms obeys the Tesco Nurture 10 (TN10) certification scheme for producers supplying Tesco UK with fresh fruits and fresh vegetables by obeying the following points:

Use of proper pesticides

Use of fertilizers

Prevention of harmful effects on the environment

Protection of health

Efficient use of energy, water, and other natural resources

Recycling of materials

Conservation of the natural environment at the production site
